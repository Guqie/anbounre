# Comic Storyboard: Turing Test

## Style
Art: ligne-claire
Tone: neutral

## Page 1: The Proposal
**Panel**: Medium shot of Alan Turing.
**Action**: Turing is standing in front of a chalkboard with "The Imitation Game" written on it. He looks directly at the viewer, holding a piece of chalk.
**Caption**: 1950年，艾伦·图灵提出了一个著名的思想实验。
**Visuals**: 1950s classroom/lab, tweed suit, chalkboard, math equations.

## Page 2: The Setup
**Panel**: Diagrammatic view / Isometric view.
**Action**: A cutaway view of three separate rooms. Room C (center) has the Interrogator typing. Room A (left) has a Man typing. Room B (right) has a Woman typing. Cables connect the machines.
**Caption**: 他设想了一个模仿游戏：一个男人（A）、一个女人（B）和一个询问者（C）。询问者C被关在一个房间里，只能通过打字交流。
**Visuals**: Teleprinters, cables, isolated rooms, "A", "B", "C" labels on doors.

## Page 3: The Question
**Panel**: Split screen or juxtaposition.
**Action**: Left side: The Man (A) is replaced by a large, bulky 1950s computer mainframe. Right side: The Interrogator (C) looks at a printout with a confused expression.
**Caption**: 图灵问：如果机器取代了A或B的位置，询问者C还能分辨出谁是人、谁是机器吗？这就是著名的“图灵测试”。
**Visuals**: Glowing vacuum tubes on the machine, paper tape, confusion, question marks.
