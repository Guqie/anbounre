# Character Sheet: Turing Story
Style: ligne-claire, clean lines, flat colors, european comic style (Tintin).
Tone: neutral, academic, 1950s setting.

## Main Characters

### Alan Turing
- **Role**: The narrator/scientist.
- **Appearance**: British man, late 30s, short dark hair parted on side, clean shaven.
- **Outfit**: Brown tweed suit jacket, tie, dress shirt.
- **Expression**: Thoughtful, intelligent, slight smile.
- **Props**: Sometimes holding a paper or standing near a chalkboard.

### The Interrogator (C)
- **Role**: The judge of the imitation game.
- **Appearance**: Gender-neutral or generic male figure, seen from back or side often.
- **Outfit**: Lab coat or 1950s office wear.
- **Setting**: Isolated in a room with a teleprinter/typewriter.
